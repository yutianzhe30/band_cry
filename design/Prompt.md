  English Prompt:
  > A messy and cramped music rehearsal room in a basement. A drum kit is in the corner, a guitar leans against a peeling 
  wall covered in band posters, and cables are tangled on the floor. Black and white manga style, high contrast with heavy
  shadows and dynamic screentone patterns. The atmosphere is full of youthful energy and creative chaos, in the nostalgic 
  art style of 90s anime like "我为歌狂". Low-angle shot.

  中文提示:
  >
  位于地下室的、杂乱而拥挤的音乐排练室。角落里放着一套架子鼓，一把吉他斜靠在贴满乐队海报的斑驳墙壁上，电线在地上缠绕。黑白
  漫画风格，高对比度，有浓重的阴影和动感的网点纸图案。整体氛围充满了青春的活力和创造性的混沌感，采用类似《我为歌狂》的90年
  代怀旧动画艺术风格。低角度拍摄。
